## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Use ENS names** - Say "vitalik.eth" instead of the full address
2. **Specify network** - Add "on Base" or "on Arbitrum" for specific chains
3. **Be conversational** - The AI understands natural language
4. **Ask follow-ups** - Continue the conversation for more details
5. **Compare things** - Ask to compare prices, gas, or protocols
